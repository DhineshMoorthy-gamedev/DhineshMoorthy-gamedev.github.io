A WebGL racing game where the player drives alongside multiple AI-controlled cars on a predefined circuit. The project integrates Photon Fusion in Shared Mode to enable real-time multiplayer gameplay, allowing multiple players to join the same race while competing against AI drivers.

The system synchronizes car positions, physics, and lap progress across all clients, ensuring smooth and consistent gameplay. AI cars continue to race in parallel, creating a dynamic and competitive environment where both human and AI racers share the same track with realistic overtaking, collisions, and circuit navigation.

Genre: Multiplayer Racing / AI Simulation
Platform: WebGL (Browser)
Team Size: Solo Project
Tools: Unity, Photon Fusion (Shared Mode), C# (AI Behavior & Networking)
